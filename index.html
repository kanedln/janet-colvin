A Legacy of Educational Service

My journey in education began in the classrooms of Milwaukee, where I first witnessed the transformative power of reciprocal teaching and culturally relevant pedagogy. Since then, my path has led me from the front lines of instruction to the executive offices of school leadership.

As a licensed Principal, Director of Instruction, and Director of Special Education, I have spent my career navigating the intersections of curriculum and administrative strategy. Whether I am implementing a Universal Breakfast program to meet basic needs or securing national recognition from the Expeditionary Learning Curriculum, my focus remains the same: the child.

In 2022, I was honored with a Mayoral Proclamation for "Janet Colvin Day," recognizing my work in building improvements, curricular innovation, and the establishment of a Science Research Dome. I am currently working on research in Leadership and Service, continuing my lifelong quest to refine the systems that serve our youth.
